Lezo zinyawo, nalolo phimbo, kwangu yi WAYA WAYA, umphakathi meseli omkhulu kwa ngelase Phola. Yona ya qala nguye u Lucas Chippa Mtshweni
Indoda e zalelwe ya phinde ya khuliswa khona lapha e Phola, yona e thanda konke oku phila nayo nokuzo landela.


I WAYA WAYA, ya suka yaba li qhawe labantu base Phola, iba sekele futhi iba lekelela lapho be hluleka khona
Ya qala kancinci, kodwa namu hlanje isisi yenzele uku khulu, oku nga qhazeki ngama gama. Thola apha e nzansi ngoku landelana izinto i
WAYA WAYA esi yenzele kona i Phola ngale zinsuku ezi hlanu i phila.

  • Ya buyisa umhlaba e kuba hlali base Phola
  • Ya khulumisana na Mayini ase duze kuba kube nemisebenzi.
  • Yaletha Umbani na Manzi kulabo ebenge nawo
  • Ya qhosha u masipala obe nga si sizi.
  • Wa hlanganisa o somashishini ukuba bezo khulisa i Kasi labo

The first thing he did was negotiate with a man known to me as "Nyawane" to allow the unoccupied land in Phola to claimed by the community
at first he was reluctant to do so but as with all things being pursued by WAYA WAYA he eventually came to realize its in his
best interest to do so.



He then went on to make sure the community can sustain those jobs by trying to create financial stability for them through job creation
Again he didnt go far but instead looked around and saw opportunity in his surrounding environment.

Thats when he started negotiating with the surrounding mines for job creativity and when they were dragging their feet in
in creating those jobs he called the community ad organized a legal strike through which no operations would take place
at those mines until they started to see the urgency or his requests. Eventually they understood and positions were opened.


The next mission came about when he realized that some residents of Phola had water or electricity for a substantial amount of time
He then started negotiations with the MEC to have those services rendered to them as they currently are in the process of installing them.


Seeing the achievements he had made under such a short space of time, achievements our government could not do with all those years
The community decided to exclude itself from the emmalahleni municipality, as it is now standing alone with an idea of a new municipality
one with the name Winnie Madikizela Municipality The last thing he did was t create a creative sapce for problem solving, which birthed the WAYA WAYA entrepreneurs division.
Which tackles unemployment, services delivery and the overall state of living in community. In close collaboration with the people around it.

Ya qala kancane ngezi ncwadi, zi cela ushintxo, izithumelwa zi cela u a manzi no gezi
Kodwa azange basithathele e nhloko, becabanga kuba siya dlala, ukuba siza thula, kodwa bengazi ukuba
Uku qabanga kwethu seku xintxile, kuba asisese mathongo engena zinqondo aba waziyo, sikhule ukunya
Sa qala sa shukumisa umzabalazo, kwa valwa indawo yabo yoku sebenzela, kwango vulwa kufika sithola impendulo, kodwa bangesi thathela enhloko futhi.



Uma into inge sebenzi, uyayi lahla, kanjalo nathi sasebenzisa loyo mqondo kubo
